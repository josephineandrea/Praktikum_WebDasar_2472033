About Bikini Bottom

Bikini Bottom is the vibrant underwater city where SpongeBob SquarePants and his friends live. From the Krusty Krab to Jellyfish Fields, there's always something fun happening here!

Top Attractions

- Krusty Krab: Home of the famous Krabby Patty. Dont forget to by one!
- Jellyfish Fields: Perfect for jellyfish catching and picnics.
- Goo Lagoon: A beautiful lagoon for swimming and sunbathing.
- Sandy's Treedome: Visit Sandy Cheeks' treedome for a taste of Texas underwater.

Travel Tips

- Bring your jellyfishing net for a fun day at Jellyfish Fields.
- Don't forget to try a Krabbby Patty at the Krusty Krab.
- Be careful around Plankton's Chum Bucket-It's not as tasty as it sounds!

About the Author

SpongeBob SquarePants is a fry cook at the Krusty Krab and a proud resident of Bikini Bottom. He loves jellyfishing, bubble blowing, and sharing his adventures with the world.
